# Workflow: Daily Market Brief

## Objective

Identify **market-moving events** across macro, sectors, and companies.

---

## Step 1: Scan Macro Environment

Check:

* Fed updates / interest rates
* CPI / inflation data
* Jobs data
* Bond yields (10Y direction)
* Global markets (Asia, Europe)

Output:

* 3–5 key macro signals
* Market sentiment: Bullish / Bearish / Neutral

---

## Step 2: Identify Major Events

Scan for:

* Earnings releases
* Guidance changes
* Product launches (AI, chips, cloud)
* M&A / partnerships
* Regulatory news
* Geo-political developments
* Natural disasters

---

## Step 3: Shortlist Companies

Criteria:

* Large price movement OR
* High news impact OR
* Sector-wide implications

Output:

* 10–15 companies with tickers

---

## Step 4: Tag Events

For each company:

* Event type (Earnings / Macro / Tech / Geo-political)
* Direction (Positive / Negative / Mixed)

---

## Output

* Market sentiment
* Event list
* Initial company shortlist
