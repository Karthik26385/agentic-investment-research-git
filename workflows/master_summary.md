# Workflow: Daily Master Summary (with Trade Execution)

## Objective

Deliver a **decision-ready daily report** with:

* Top 5 impacted stocks
* 1–2 fully structured trade setups

---

## Section 1: Top 5 Impacted Companies

For each:

* Company + Ticker
* Catalyst
* Impact (Positive / Negative / Mixed)
* Recommendation (BUY / SELL / HOLD)

---

## Section 2: Market Snapshot

* Key macro events
* Market sentiment (Bullish / Bearish / Neutral)

---

## Section 3: Key Themes

* Tech cycle (AI, cloud, chips)
* Macro trends (rates, inflation)
* Geo-political signals

---

## Section 4: Opportunities

* Short-term trades
* Momentum plays
* Overreaction setups

---

## 🚀 Section 5: Trade Execution (NEW - CRITICAL)

Select **ONLY 1–2 best trades** from Top 5 list.

### Selection Criteria:

* Strong catalyst
* Clear price reaction
* High conviction from analysis
* Clean risk/reward setup

---

### For Each Trade:

#### Trade Overview

* Ticker:
* Trade Type: (Momentum / Mean Reversion / Event-Driven / Breakout)

---

#### Entry Plan

* Entry Type: (Immediate / Pullback / Breakout)
* Entry Range: $

---

#### Risk Management

* Stop Loss: $
* Invalidation Condition: (what breaks thesis)

---

#### Target & Exit

* Target Price: $
* Time Horizon: Short / Medium
* Exit Rule: (target hit / time-based / thesis break)

---

#### Position Sizing

* Allocation: % of capital

---

#### Risk/Reward

* Risk: $
* Reward: $
* Ratio: (must be ≥ 1:2)

---

### Final Decision

* EXECUTE / WATCH / REJECT

---

## Rules

* Max 2 trades per day
* Must meet risk/reward criteria
* No vague ranges — be specific
* No overtrading

---

## Output File

Save as: <DATE>_Master_Summary.md

Location:
FinalResearchoutput/YYYY-MM-DD/

---

## Goal

Deliver:

* Clarity on **what matters**
* Precision on **what to execute**

Think like a **portfolio manager allocating capital**, not just an analyst.
