# Workflow: Event-Driven Analysis

## Objective

Analyze each selected company for **financial and market impact**

---

## Step 1: Context

* What happened?
* Why now?

---

## Step 2: Business Impact

Evaluate:

* Revenue impact
* Cost impact
* Margins
* Growth outlook

---

## Step 3: Market Reaction

* Stock move (%)
* Volume spike
* Sector reaction

---

## Step 4: Mispricing Detection

Check:

* Overreaction?
* Underreaction?
* Already priced in?

---

## Step 5: Trade Thesis

Define:

* Bull case
* Bear case

---

## Step 6: Recommendation

* BUY / SELL / HOLD
* Confidence: High / Medium / Low
* Time Horizon: Short / Medium

---

## Output Format (per company)

* Ticker
* Catalyst
* Impact
* Thesis
* Recommendation
