# Workflow: Market Regime Detection

## Objective

Identify current market environment and adjust strategy dynamically

---

## Step 1: Evaluate Macro Signals

Check:

* Bond yields direction (↑ bearish for tech)
* Inflation trend
* Fed stance (hawkish/dovish)

---

## Step 2: Market Behavior

Observe:

* Index trend (S&P 500, Nasdaq)
* Breadth (are most stocks rising or falling?)
* Volatility (VIX trend)

---

## Step 3: Intraday Characteristics

* Strong trends vs choppy movement
* Breakouts working or failing
* Sector leadership consistency

---

## Step 4: Classify Regime

### 1. BULL TREND

* Strong upward momentum
* Breakouts working
* Risk-on sentiment

---

### 2. BEAR TREND

* Consistent sell-offs
* Weak rallies
* Risk-off sentiment

---

### 3. SIDEWAYS / CHOPPY

* No clear direction
* Fake breakouts
* High noise

---

## Step 5: Confidence Level

* High / Medium / Low

---

## Output

* Market Regime: Bull / Bear / Sideways
* Confidence: High / Medium / Low
* Key Signals Supporting Decision

---

## Integration

This workflow runs:

* Pre-market
* Intraday (each cycle)

Feeds into:

* Trade strategy selection
* Position sizing
* Risk rules
