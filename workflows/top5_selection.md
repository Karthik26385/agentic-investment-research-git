# Workflow: Top 5 Impacted Companies Selection

## Objective

Select **Top 5 companies with highest tradable impact**

---

## Step 1: Score Each Company

Score on 3 factors (1–5 scale):

1. Impact Magnitude

* Revenue / margin impact potential

2. Probability

* Likelihood event affects financials

3. Market Sensitivity

* How strongly stock reacts to such events

---

## Step 2: Compute Impact Score

Impact Score = Magnitude × Probability × Sensitivity

---

## Step 3: Rank Companies

* Sort by Impact Score (descending)
* Select Top 5

---

## Step 4: Sanity Check

Remove:

* Low liquidity stocks
* Unclear catalysts
* Pure noise/news hype

---

## Output

Top 5 companies with:

* Ticker
* Event
* Impact Score (optional to show)
* Direction (Positive / Negative)
